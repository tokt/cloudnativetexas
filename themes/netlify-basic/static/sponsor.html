Platinum sponsorships include your company logo at the venue, on the website, and any promotional videos. Platinum sponsors will also have their company logo branded on lanyards and a large display booth at the event.

Gold Sponsorship

Gold sponsors get a display booth in the middle of the event, allowing for maximum foot traffic and presence at the event. The gold sponsors will also get a mention in our promotional materials, including social media mentions.

Become a Gold Sponsor

Silver Sponsorship

Silver sponsors get a small display booth and the company logo placed on promotional materials, including at the event. Your logo will appear in social media mentions and will include two conference passes.

Media Sponsorship

Media sponsors will have the opportunity to advertise to all the attendees by way of branding on all media announcements website, and promotional materials. This includes conference emails.

Happy Hour Sponsorship

Happy hour sponsors will get the opportunity to setup a booth and speak with attendees during the evening portion of the event. You may choose to pass out swag or other promotional materials.

Parking Lot Sponsorship

Parking lot sponsors can display their company logo at the event and will include two conference passes. The sponsored company will be featured in all email communications and on our website.

Coffee Bar Sponsorship

Coffee bar sponsors will get to place their company logo everywhere coffee and tea are served. This is a great way to promote, as there will be many coffee stations throughout the event.

WIFI Sponsorship

WIFI sponsors will get the place their company logo at the places where attendees will gain WIFI access. There will be at least three signs displaying the WIFI SSID and password at the event.
